All About Linux

Introduction to Linux

Linux is an open-source, Unix-like operating system that is widely used in servers, desktops, and mobile devices. It is known for its stability, security, and flexibility. Linux is used by many developers and companies, making it one of the most popular operating systems in the world.

Features of Linux

Linux offers a range of features that make it a powerful OS for both personal and professional use:

  • Open-source software
  • Free to use
  • Highly customizable
  • Extensive software support
  • Security features, such as SELinux
  • Stability and performance

Why Use Linux?

Linux is used by millions of people around the world, from casual users to large enterprises. Here are some reasons why you should consider using Linux:

  • Linux is free and open-source, meaning you can download, modify, and distribute it without any licensing fees.
  • Linux is extremely secure and is less prone to viruses and malware compared to other operating systems.
  • It supports a wide range of software, including development tools, servers, and entertainment apps.
